The SciGlob/NASA team typically manages 15+ instruments simultaneously through this process. The realistic intake-to-shipment timeline is 4–8 months per instrument depending on queue position, calibration bench availability, weather at the clearance site, and data quality requirements. The GSFC Roof Clearance phase alone spans 2–3 months of calendar time. The hours listed represent actual dedicated labor per instrument, not elapsed calendar time.
